.Click

Upon clicking, the background of this div row will change to solid yellow. Ouch.

When you click something, something happens. The End

This could be used to start battles, show investigator, or choose Event

.Hide/.Show

Click '.hide' and this image will disappear.

Click '.show' and its comes back.

This could be to lose investigators in time and space

.Toggle

Upon clicking, an element will turn on or off.

The difference between this and hide/show is that you only need ONE button.

For example, the 'Toggle Game Rules' button at the top of the pages shows or hides the game rules.

"Toggle" doom track.

.SlideDown/.SlideUp

Click 'Slide Up' and the lightbulb will slide up and disappear.

Click 'Slide Up' and the lightbulb comes back.

Slide up and down the offical rules of the game from the navbar

.SlideToggle

Upon clicking, an element will slide up or down, depending on it's previous/current state.

The difference between this and up/down is that you only need ONE button.

"SlideToggle" the game map.

.FadeIn/.FadeOut

Click 'Fade In' and the monster will appear.

Click 'Fade Out' and it goes away.

.addClass

Upon clicking, a class will be added to a connected element.

For example, this button will change.

Add the 'insane' or 'unconsious' class to investigators.

.before/.after

Click 'Before' and a paragraph will appear above this one. Click 'after' and it appears below.

.append

Upon clicking, insert content, specified by the parameter, to the end of each element in the set of matched elements..
